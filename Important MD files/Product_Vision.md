# QMOS - Community Operating System

## Vision

QMOS (Community Operating System) is a centralized platform designed to help student communities, clubs, and organizations manage events, members, quizzes, announcements, resources, certificates, and engagement from a single place.

Instead of using multiple tools like WhatsApp, Google Forms, Excel Sheets, and separate quiz platforms, QMOS brings everything together into one lightweight and easy-to-use system.

---

# Problem Statement

Most student communities face common challenges:

- Event registrations are managed through Google Forms.
- Attendance is tracked manually.
- Announcements get lost in WhatsApp groups.
- Quiz platforms are disconnected from community activities.
- Certificates are generated manually.
- Community leaders cannot track member engagement.
- Students miss important updates and opportunities.

As communities grow, managing all these activities becomes difficult and time-consuming.

QMOS solves these problems by providing one unified platform.

# Core Features

A personalized dashboard showing:

- Quiz opportunities

## Event Management

Communities can:

- Track attendance

## Quiz Platform

- Fun quizzes
- Event competitions
